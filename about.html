Our Story

a safe haven dedicated to helping adolescents navigate the challenges of stress and anxiety. Our journey began with a deep concern for the mental health of young people, particularly inspired by the ongoing struggles in Gaza. Witnessing the immense pressure faced by adolescents in such turbulent times ignited our passion to create a platform that offers support not only to those in Gaza but to young people worldwide. We aim to provide the tools and resources necessary to manage stress effectively and build resilience.



We chose the name "Violet Lavenders" for our website because of the profound meanings behind both the color violet and the lavender plant. In color theory, violet symbolizes calm, creativity, and wisdom. It has a soothing effect on the mind and body, promoting a sense of peace and reducing stress. Lavender, known for its aromatic properties, has been used for centuries to alleviate anxiety, promote relaxation, and improve sleep. Together, the color violet and the benefits of lavender encapsulate our mission to provide a calming and supportive environment for adolescents facing stress and anxiety.

Who we target

We specifically target adolescents aged 13 to 20, recognizing that this transition period is fraught with unique challenges. According to global statistics, approximately 10-20% of adolescents experience mental health conditions, and depression is a leading cause of illness and disability among them. The pressures of academic performance, social dynamics, and personal development can lead to significant stress and anxiety. Our mission is to address these issues head-on, providing a supportive environment where young people can find solace and guidance .



What We Offer

Our website operates by offering a variety of engaging and therapeutic activities designed to alleviate stress. Users can explore options like reading, engaging in physical workouts, participating in worship, journaling, meditating, and even gaming.
